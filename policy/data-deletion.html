How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by emailing privacy@natureswaysoil.com with the subject Data Deletion Request. Include any identifiers relevant to your request.

What We Delete

  • Personal identifiers associated with analytics data, where present.
  • Any stored access tokens or artifacts tied to your identity.

What We Retain

Aggregated, non-personal advertising performance metrics may be retained for business reporting, where they cannot reasonably identify an individual.

Verification

We may require proof of identity to protect against unauthorized deletion requests.

Timeline

We aim to process eligible deletion requests within 30 days.
