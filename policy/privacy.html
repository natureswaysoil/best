Effective Date: 2025-01-01

1. Overview

Nature's Way Soil values your privacy. This dashboard visualizes Amazon PPC performance data. We minimize the personal data we process and use secure, privacy-respecting practices.

2. Data We Process

  • Advertising performance metrics from Amazon Ads, stored in Google BigQuery.
  • Technical logs (anonymized) for security and diagnostics.
  • No end-user account data is collected by the dashboard.

3. Data Sources

  • Amazon Ads API (campaign, keyword, and performance metrics).
  • Google BigQuery (data warehouse for analytics).

4. How We Use Data

  • To render dashboards and insights for internal business purposes.
  • To monitor and improve the performance and reliability of the service.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers (e.g., Google Cloud) strictly to operate the service, under appropriate agreements.

6. Security

We use least-privilege IAM, HTTPS-only transport, and do not store credentials in the repository. Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel.

7. Retention

Advertising performance data is retained as needed for reporting and optimization, then deleted or archived according to internal policies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. See the Data Deletion page for instructions.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries or requests, email: privacy@natureswaysoil.com
