# NWS_014 Dog Urine Neutralizer — Better Product Video Brief

## Goal
Create a clean, trust-building short video that sells the product without looking spammy, AI-generated, or confusing.

## Recommended length
18–24 seconds.

## Format
Vertical 9:16 for TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Winning angle
Your dog is not the problem. The soil needs repair.

## Visual style
Real lawn, real bottle, simple text overlays, natural outdoor light. Avoid fake before/after images, robotic avatars, or cluttered backgrounds.

## Shot list
1. 0–3 sec — Hook
   - Visual: close-up of yellow dog urine lawn spot.
   - Text: Your dog is not the problem.

2. 3–6 sec — Problem
   - Visual: hand points to yellow grass / soil area.
   - Text: Urine salts build up in the soil.

3. 6–10 sec — Product reveal
   - Visual: Nature's Way Soil Dog Urine Neutralizer bottle on grass.
   - Text: Treat the soil, not just the color.

4. 10–15 sec — Application
   - Visual: spray product on affected area, then water in.
   - Text: Spray. Water in. Let grass recover.

5. 15–20 sec — Trust
   - Visual: dog walking safely near lawn, bottle nearby.
   - Text: Pet-safe. Not a dye.

6. 20–24 sec — CTA
   - Visual: bottle + healthy lawn background.
   - Text: Save 15% on your first order.
   - CTA: Shop direct at natureswaysoil.com/lawn-repair
